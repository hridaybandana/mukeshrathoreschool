Help & Support

We are here to guide and support parents & students

General Assistance

Mukesh Rathore School is committed to providing timely and transparent support to parents and students. Our administrative and academic teams are available during school working hours to resolve queries related to admissions, academics, fees, and general school matters.

Admissions Help

The school provides complete assistance for new admissions including eligibility, required documents, admission timelines, and fee structure. Parents are encouraged to visit the school office or the Admissions section of the website for accurate guidance.

Fee & Payment Support

Parents can seek help regarding fee structure, installment options, payment modes, and receipt-related queries. The accounts department ensures transparency and clarity in all fee-related matters.

Academic & Student Support

Support is available for syllabus queries, examinations, competitive exam preparation, co-curricular activities, and student well-being. Parents may request meetings with teachers or the Principal through the school office.

Certificates & Documents

The school assists with issuing bonafide certificates, transfer certificates, attendance records, and academic reports upon request.

Future Online Portal

Mukesh Rathore School plans to introduce student and teacher login portals in the near future for accessing results, notices, and academic records.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What classes does the school offer? +
The school offers education from Class I to Class X.
What curriculum does the school follow? +
Mukesh Rathore School follows the CBSE curriculum.
What are the school timings? +
The school operates from 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday to Saturday.
How can parents pay school fees? +
Fees can be paid through online or offline modes as informed by the school.
Is there a fee discount for lump sum payment? +
Yes, a discount may be available if the full fee is paid at admission.
Does the school offer competitive exam preparation? +
Yes, foundation-level preparation for Olympiads and competitive exams is provided.
How can parents meet teachers? +
Parents can meet teachers during PTMs or by scheduling an appointment.
Are sports compulsory? +
Yes, sports and physical education are integral parts of the curriculum.
Is medical support available? +
Basic first-aid and medical assistance is available during school hours.
How can parents contact the school? +
Parents can contact the school office via phone or email during working hours.
