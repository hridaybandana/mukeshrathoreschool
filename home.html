About the School


Mukesh Rathore School is committed to providing a nurturing and inclusive environment where students can thrive in all aspects of their development. Our primary focus is on ensuring academic excellence, character building, and overall growth, with the goal of preparing students for future success in both their personal and professional lives.

At Mukesh Rathore School, we understand that education goes beyond textbooks. While we place a strong emphasis on achieving high academic standards, we also prioritize the development of key life skills such as leadership, responsibility, and ethical values. We believe that a well-rounded student is one who not only excels in their studies but also contributes positively to society. Through various extracurricular activities, students have opportunities to explore their passions, whether it be in the arts, sports, or community service.

Our curriculum encourages students to think beyond the conventional, engage with innovative ideas, and develop solutions to real-world challenges. This helps students build resilience, adaptability, and a growth mindset, which are essential for success in today's rapidly changing world.

At Mukesh Rathore School, we believe that growth is a continuous journey. We are committed to not only shaping knowledgeable individuals but also responsible, compassionate citizens who will lead with integrity. Through a holistic approach to education, we aim to equip students with the tools they need to succeed in life and make meaningful contributions to society.


Principal's Message


Welcome to our school, a place where learning is nurtured with care, values are strengthened, and young minds are inspired to achieve excellence.

As a CBSE-affiliated institution catering to students from Class I to Class X, we are committed to providing a balanced education that focuses not only on academic achievement but also on character building, creativity, and holistic development.

In today’s rapidly changing world, education must go beyond textbooks. At our school, we encourage critical thinking, curiosity, discipline, and a lifelong love for learning.

We believe that parents and teachers are partners in a child’s educational journey. Together, we strive to instill strong moral values, social responsibility, and confidence in our students so they can face future challenges with courage and integrity.

I invite you to join us in shaping responsible, compassionate, and successful individuals who will contribute positively to society.
