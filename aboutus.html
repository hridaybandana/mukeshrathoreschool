About Mukesh Rathore School

Building Knowledge, Character & Confidence Since 2007

Our History

Mukesh Rathore School was established in the year 2007 with a clear vision to provide quality education that balances academic excellence with strong moral values. What began as a modest institution with a small group of students has steadily grown into a well-recognized school serving learners from Class I to Class X.

Over the years, the school has continuously evolved by adopting modern teaching methodologies while preserving traditional values of discipline, respect, and responsibility.

With consistent academic results, growing infrastructure, and a dedicated teaching staff, Mukesh Rathore School has earned the trust of parents and the respect of the community. The institution remains committed to nurturing confident, responsible, and future-ready individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Staff

The strength of Mukesh Rathore School lies in its dedicated and diverse team of staff members, who work together to create a safe, disciplined, and inspiring learning environment.

The school is led by an experienced Principal, whose leadership focuses on academic excellence, student welfare, and continuous improvement. The Principal’s cabin remains open to students and parents for guidance, suggestions, and addressing concerns.

Our teaching faculty consists of trained and passionate educators specializing in their respective subjects. Teachers focus not only on syllabus completion but also on concept clarity, creativity, and individual attention. Regular workshops and training sessions help them stay updated with modern teaching techniques.

The school is supported by skilled laboratory assistants who ensure safe and effective practical learning in science and computer labs. Physical Education (PT) teachers and Yoga instructors guide students toward physical fitness, discipline, and mental well-being through structured activities and daily practice.

Equally important are our non-teaching staff, including peons, administrative staff, and the cleaning department. They play a vital role in maintaining cleanliness, safety, and smooth day-to-day operations.

Together, the entire team works with dedication, empathy, and professionalism to support students in their academic journey and personal growth.

School Building & Infrastructure

Mukesh Rathore School is designed to provide a safe, spacious, and student-friendly environment. The campus includes well-ventilated classrooms equipped with proper seating and learning aids.

The school has separate science laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, allowing students to perform experiments and understand concepts practically.

A fully functional computer laboratory with internet-enabled systems supports digital learning and computer education. The sports room stores equipment for indoor and outdoor games, while a dedicated yoga room provides a peaceful space for meditation and physical wellness activities.

The Principal’s cabin is centrally located and open to parents and students for queries, feedback, and grievance redressal. Additional facilities include staff rooms, administrative offices, library, medical room, drinking water stations, and clean washrooms for students and staff.

Facilities Available

  • Smart and well-lit classrooms
  • Science laboratories (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Computer lab with modern systems
  • Library with academic and reference books
  • Sports and indoor games facilities
  • Yoga and meditation room
  • Safe drinking water and clean sanitation
  • Medical and first-aid facility
  • CCTV surveillance and secure campus

Competitive Exam Preparation

In addition to regular curriculum teaching, Mukesh Rathore School provides early exposure to competitive examinations. Teachers guide students in developing logical reasoning, analytical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Special focus is given to preparing students for Olympiads, scholarship exams, and foundation-level competitive tests.

Sports Curriculum

Sports and physical education are an integral part of the school curriculum. Students participate in regular PT classes, yoga sessions, and organized sports activities. The curriculum focuses on physical fitness, teamwork, discipline, and leadership. Inter-house and inter-school competitions encourage healthy competition and sportsmanship among students.

School Location

Mukesh Rathore School is located in a safe and easily accessible area of Indore, making it convenient for students and parents. The school is well-connected by road and public transport, ensuring smooth daily commuting.
